FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest storyline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0. This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson, who scored 13 goals and still holds the record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat cursed American Samoan football, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, Next Goal Wins, showing across the world.
